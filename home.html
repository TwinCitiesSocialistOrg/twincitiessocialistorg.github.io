Twin Cities Socialists

Hey there, we're the Twin Cities branch of the International Socialist Organization (ISO)! We're dedicated to building socialist politics within the Twin Cities metro area. If you want to learn more about the ISO nationally as an organization look at our about page.

Upcoming Public Events

If you want to come to a branch meeting check out the events page. If you're looking for ways to stay up-to-date on the latest in movement news, political analysis or just want to learn more then be sure to check out our resources page.

Feel free to contact us with your questions, concerns or invitations to collaborate organizationally. We also have an email list you can join.
