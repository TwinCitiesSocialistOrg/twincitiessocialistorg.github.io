The Fight For Women's Lives!

Tuesday, October 16th
6:30pm - 8:30pm

Room 1114 Social Sciences Bldg.
West Bank Campus - University of Minnesota,
Minneapolis, MN

From Trump to Kavanaugh, it could not be clearer that our lives don't matter -- not to anyone running this society. But it also could not be clearer that we know that they do, and we are going to fight for what we deserve.

What will it take for our rage and determination to solidify into something real? Come to a talk and discussion about #MeToo and more -- above all, how we can build a women's movement that can win.
